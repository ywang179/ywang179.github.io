• 红日生产大队历程
  1. 建设伊始 Early Construction (2018)
    红日生产大队概念最初产生于2018年寒假适应性学习时期（约2018年2月4日左右，具体时间不可考）。此时得益于没有作业压力的社会背景，以及处于美术教室自习的优越地理位置，巨鸭艮和贤鱼开始了对于“红日生产大队”的蓝图建设。红日生产队最初的生产模式基于生物选修三课本中对于“桑基鱼塘”概念的介绍，并由此开发出一款适生腌萝卜、咸鸭蛋与咸鱼的盐碱可循环版“丧基鱼塘”。“丧”正是企业建设初期的文化核心。贤鱼与巨鸭艮利用身边的人脉资源，将各自的同桌拉入伙，形成红日核心四人组，即：贤鱼（大队长）、巨鸭艮（二把手）、八千（资方金主）、小鸡（吉祥物）；并推出了当时的主打产品：鲱鱼罐头、咸鸭蛋以及欧防风。
    The concept of the Red Sun Production Team emerged around February 4, 2018, during a relaxed winter break. Benefiting from a study space in the art room and a lack of academic pressure, two characters—Giant Goose and Wise Fish—started planning the “Red Sun Production Team.” The initial production model was inspired by the concept of “mulberry-dyke fish ponds” in biology textbooks, leading to the development of a salt-alkali recyclable system named “Mourning Fish Pond” for preserved radish, salted eggs, and salted fish. “Mourning” was, indeed, the core of Red Sun’s early corporate culture. Using their connections, Wise Fish and Giant Goose recruited their desk mates, forming the Red Sun core four: Wise Fish (team captain), Giant Goose (second-in-command), Eight Thousand (investor), and Little Chick (mascot). Together, they launched the flagship products: canned herring, salted eggs, and parsnip.
  2. 规模扩大 Expansion (2019)
    随着红日农产品的推广，对其他物种与工种的需求也增加了。红日在种植农业的基础上，增加了农家乐与养老服务，提升企业的竞争力。并通过招商引资，吸纳了室友和同学等大量技术人才与优质物种。
    此时因为人丁兴旺，巨鸭艮会时常组织名为“阅读沙龙”的活动，促进成员之间的交流。与前期相比，兴盛的红日生产大队的基调不再“丧”，而因为多元和脱离幻想变得更加务实和快乐。
    但繁荣的经济也会引来他人觊觎。红日不但拥有了名为绿月的竞争对手（现已破产合并），还发现了如蛙青、tori鲶、魔法师等反派角色，其中蛙青对于生产队的打击是巨大的，曾通过抄没的手段，毁灭性击垮了红日的生产资料，并导致了一次红日成员大迁徙。
    As Red Sun’s agricultural products gained popularity, so did the need for more species and roles. To stay competitive, Red Sun expanded into agritourism and elder care services, leveraging its popularity to recruit talent from among classmates. With the influx of new members, Giant Goose organized “reading salons” to foster connections. No longer merely “mourning,” Red Sun became pragmatic and joyful, reflecting its diverse and down-to-earth approach. Prosperity, however, attracted rivals, such as “Green Moon” (later bankrupted and merged) and adversaries like Frog Green and Tori the Catfish. Frog Green’s sabotage even led to a massive exodus from Red Sun after seizing production assets.
  3. 陷入沉寂 Quiet Years (2020-2022)
    在历经一场名为“高烤”的极端天气之后，因为酷暑难耐，大部分红日成员选择北上，各自发展。巨鸭艮试图在华东设立分厂，但推销几度遇冷。期间巨鸭艮与贤鱼通过网络与交通手段，间或保持联系。但因为学业压力与疫情的社会环境，红日的发展基本进入停滞阶段。
    巨鸭艮在此阶段自产自销了几位新成员，包括小柴糊（狗姐）、烛龙等，并优化完善了内部的人物关系网，对人物信息进行追溯考察。此外巨鸭艮基于风景园林专业的课程设计，对乡村农业旅游业发展与景观营造有了更深远的认知。而贤鱼就读于经管专业（大概吧我分不清楚商院的专业），为红日企业的转型与长远发展提供可行可靠的理论依据。
    此阶段贤鱼与巨鸭艮发现相比摸鱼与挖坑，彼此更擅长画饼。
    After enduring an extreme weather event dubbed the “High Roast,” most Red Sun members dispersed, seeking cooler climates. Giant Goose attempted to establish a branch in East China, but met with lukewarm reception. Due to academic pressures and the pandemic, Red Sun’s growth stagnated. During this time, Giant Goose cultivated a few new members like Dog Sis and Zhulong, improving the team’s character backstories. Giant Goose also gained a deeper understanding of rural tourism, while Wise Fish’s management studies provided theoretical support for future development. The two realized that they excelled more in painting rosy pictures than actual production.
  4. 分厂建立 Branch Establishment (2023-2024)
    在又一次经历“毕夜”之后，红日生产大队迎来新的曙光。巨鸭艮意外与旧识莫念重逢，两条龙倍感亲切，并以此为契机开展了红日华东分厂。华东分厂的调性契合上海当地需求品味，主打高端精致，主营产品包括咖啡（甜品）以及鲜花，并拥有了完整的冷链保鲜与产品回收系统，更加经济环保。
    红日企业同时优化了现有产业与服务。不但设计出层出不穷的可循环赚钱小妙招，如对染色小鸡的多轮利用回收、利用甜咸口产品营销进行打投；还更加关注养老院老人的生活品质与心理需求，定期安排保健品团队上门慰问提供温暖服务，并采用劳逸结合的方法丰富老人的老年时光。
    After another “Graduation Night,” Red Sun Production Team saw new hope. A chance reunion between Giant Goose and an old friend, Dragon Mo, led to the establishment of the East China branch. Tailored to Shanghai’s refined tastes, this branch focused on premium goods such as coffee, desserts, and flowers, boasting a complete cold chain and recycling system. Red Sun also optimized its services, launching creative money-saving hacks like multi-use, dyed chicks and integrating marketing tactics with sweet and savory products. They paid more attention to the elderly in nursing homes, frequently sending the wellness team to deliver warmth, ensuring that residents’ lives were both leisurely and enriched.
